The Story Behind Mike W Consulting

I've spent over 20 years in technology — starting in hands-on IT and systems work, and spending the last several years designing and building full software products of my own. That combination is exactly what I bring to custom software work: I understand infrastructure, security, and how systems actually run in production, not just how to write code.
